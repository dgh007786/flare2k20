About - FLARE

Widely loved and the most awaited fest of the year, Flare, is Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University’s annual socio-cultural extravaganza. It is celebrated through an exuberant amalgamation of the dynamic spheres of art, literature, music, dance, theatre and even food! Flare is a gathering of enthusiastic individuals who are provided with a sublime platform to showcase their talents, learn and grow through a wholesome euphoric experience. With performances by renowned personalities of the entertainment industry, engaging competitions and exciting events, Flare’20 commits to leave you with everlasting memories of an unforgettable episode.
